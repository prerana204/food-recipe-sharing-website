Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Minced beef - 100g
  • Tomato sauce - 1 cup
Steps:
  • Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  • Cook beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  • Combine and serve hot.
