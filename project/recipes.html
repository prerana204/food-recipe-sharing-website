Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Steps:
  • Boil fettuccine pasta until al dente.
  • In a pan, melt butter and add garlic.
  • Add heavy cream and grated Parmesan.
  • Simmer until thick and creamy.
  • Mix in pasta and serve with parsley.

Fresh Garden Salad

Steps:
  • Chop lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onions.
  • Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, and salt.
  • Add feta cheese and olives if desired.
  • Mix well and serve chilled.
